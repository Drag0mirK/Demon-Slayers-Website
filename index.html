Demon Slayer

KIMETSU NO YABA

Anime introduction

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese fantasy manga series and comic book by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy living in Taisho-era Japan who becomes the sole survivor of his family after demons kill them. He then sets out to save his sister Nezuko who has transformed into a demon.

About

Anime Story

A true phenomenon of the new manga/animated generation, Demon Slayer is a staple of Japanese pop culture. First published in 2015, Demon Slayer, or Kimetsu no Yaiba in Japanese, quickly made a name for itself on the Japanese scene before conquering the international scene. Today we introduce you to this manga whose popularity has exploded in recent years.

Anime History

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge.Demon Slayer focuses on Tanjiro Kamado, who, after venturing down from his mountainside home to sell charcoal, returns to discover his entire family has been slaughtered – except for his sister Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon.

Slayers

Hashira

Yorichi

Yoriichi is considered the strongest Demon Slayer ever to exist. He is so powerful that the battle doll crafted in his image needed multiple arms to replicate his hypersonic movements.

Kanoe

Tanjiro

Tanjiro Kamado became a Demon Slayer to hunt down the demon who murdered his family and turned his sister into a demon. A rarity among the other Slayers, Tanjiro empathizes with his demon enemies. He always kills them, but understands that most demons had no choice when they were transformed.

Hashira

Gyomei

Gyomei Himejima is the Stone Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps. Instead of the typical Nichirin Blades that Demon Slayers use, Gyomei wields a devastatingly strong hand-ax with a spiked flail attached to the hilt by a long chain.

Hashira

Sanemi

Sanemi is very hot-headed and harbors a deep hatred for demons, but it's understandable given how many people he's lost over the years. Sanemi's body is also covered in scars accumulated over his many years of slaying demons.

Hashira

Rengoku

Kyojuro Rengoku is the cheerful Flame Hashira. His Flame Breathing Style drowns his opponents in a sea of flames. He even agreed to take Tanjiro under his wing and train him prior to the events of Mugen Train.

Hashira

Tomioka

Giyu Tomioka was the first Demon Slayer Tanjiro ever met. After witnessing a newly transformed Nezuko protect Tanjiro, Tomioka points them to Urokodaki and the Demon Slayer Corps. Tomioka may not be the best with social interactions, but his fellow Hashira recognize him as one of the strongest in the group.

Hashira

Kanroji

Mitsuri Kanroji is the Love Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps. She is shy, gentle, bubbly, and has eight times the muscle density of an average human, making her wickedly strong despite her thin limbs. Her strength is so incredible that she can withstand attacks that would tear the average human body apart.

Hashira

Iguro

While Shinobu is regarded as the weakest Hashira, Obanai is known as the second-weakest. Unlike Shinobu, who never activated her Demon Slayer mark, Obanai could activate his. This allowed him to fight at peak performance, even when he lost his vision.

Hashira

Uzui

Originally from a shinobi family, Tengen Uzui is a self-titled flashy slayer of demons. Tengen didn't agree with his father's ideology of using people as tools, so he left and joined the Demon Slayer Corps, becoming the Sound Hashira.

Hashira

Tokito

Muichiro Tokito may seem a bit air-headed, always lost in his thoughts, but he's a dedicated Demon Slayer and one of the most powerful characters in the series. As the Mist Hashira, he utilizes the Mist Breathing Style derived from the Wind Breathing Style.

Kanoe

Zenitsu

Zenitsu Agatsuma claims he never wanted to become a Demon Slayer and was forced into it to pay off a debt. He's always asking girls to marry him so he can get out of being a Demon Slayer and live a long life, but this cowardly hero shouldn't be underestimated.

Kanoe

Inosuke

Inosuke Hashibira is one of the main characters in the Demon Slayer anime series. He is a young boy who wears a boar's head as a mask and wields two sharp swords. He has a wild and abrasive personality, often engaging in fights and showing a strong desire to prove his strength. Despite his rough exterior, Inosuke has a strong sense of loyalty and compassion for his friends, making him a beloved character in the series.

Demons

Upper Moon 1

Kokushibo

Kokushibo is part of the Twelve Demon Moons and has the rank of Upper Moon One - the strongest of all of the Moons in the Kimetsu no Yaiba series. Before becoming a Demon, he was called Michikatsu Tsugikuni and was part of the Demon Slayer Corps.He is the crator of Moon Breathing.

Upper Moon 2

Doma

Doma's origin story is shrouded in mystery, but we do know that he was turned into a demon by Muzan over a century before the events of the anime series began. Initially an Upper Moon Six, Doma was later promoted to Upper Moon Two for reasons unknown. With his War Fans and powerful Cryokinesis Blood Art, Doma is a formidable foe capable of taking down even the toughest of Hashira.

Upper Moon 3

Akaza

Akaza is a stubborn demon who respects the strong and despises the weak. So much so that the demon goes out of his way to challenge powerful fighters and demon slayers. Whenever Akaza would face off against opponents he deemed worthy, he would ask for their names and even introduce himself as a form of respect. He remembers the name of every opponent he has fought against throughout his life as a demon.

Upper Moon 4

Hantengu

Hantengu is one of the demons in the Demon Slayer: Swordsmith Village Arc. He is also the Upper Rank Four Demon Moon of the Twelve Kizuki, serving and reporting directly to Muzan Kibutsuji. Though always appearing weak and scared out of his wits, Hantengu contains a power to summon powerful demons which represent his many emotions.

Upper Moon 5

Gyokko

Gyokko specifically holds the position of Upper Moon Five, making him one of the more powerful demons in the series. His appearance is distinctive, with a body covered in a shell-like armor and a lantern-like structure on his head. Each member of the Twelve Kizuki is associated with a specific moon, and Gyokko's rank as Upper Moon Five signifies his strength and standing among the demons.

Upper Moon 6

Daki & Gyutaro

Daki is a beautiful and seemingly human woman who is actually a demon. Daki has the ability to manipulate her sash, which she uses both for combat and to control her victims. Gyutaro is Daki's younger brother, and he takes on the appearance of a massive demon with two heads. He is extremely protective of his sister. While Gyutaro can be ruthless in battle, he is not without a sense of loyalty and familial connection.

Lower Moon 1

Enmu

Enmu is a demon and one of the Twelve Kizuki, the upper-ranking members of the Demon Slayer Corps. Specifically, Enmu holds the position of Lower Rank One, making him one of the highest-ranking demons in the series.What makes Enmu notable is his ability to enter and manipulate the dreams of others, creating nightmarish scenarios to trap and prey upon his victims.

Lower Moon 2

Rokuro

Rokuro is a minor character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. As a Lower Rank Two of the Twelve Demon Moons, he was presumably the second strongest of the Lower Ranks, but was still not strong enough to spare himself from Muzan Kibutsuji's wrath for being unable to match the powers of the much more formidable Upper Ranks.

Lower Moon 3

Wakuraba

Out of the Lower Ranks, Wakuraba was shown to be one of the most loyal to Muzan Kibutsuji. Despite the fact that Muzan was brutally executing two of his fellow Lower Ranks, he thought only about improving himself to live up to Muzan's expectations and win back his favor. However, Wakuraba appeared to believe in self-preservation above all else and was not just blindly obedient to Muzan. He didn’t wish to be killed by him even though he clearly usually respected his leader's decisions, running away upon realizing that Muzan had no intention of letting any of the Lower Ranks be left alive.

Lower Moon 4

Mukago

Mukago was a member of the Twelve Kizuki, having held the position of Lower Rank Four.Like most demons, Mukago held great fear and respect for Muzan Kibutsuji. However, out of all the Lower Ranks, she was also shown to be the most cowardly. As stated by Muzan through reading her thoughts, Mukago had no intention of battling the Demon Slayer Corps' Hashira and planned to flee at the mere mention of one, noted to have done so numerous times. When Muzan learned of this, Mukago attempted to deny her thoughts in an attempt to appease him.

Lower Moon 5

Rui

Rui is a powerful demon in "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" who holds the rank of Lower Rank Five among the Twelve Kizuki. He has the ability to control deadly threads and is driven by a desire to create a perfect family. Rui plays a central role in the Natagumo Mountain Arc, where he becomes a formidable opponent for the main characters, Tanjiro and Nezuko.

Lower Moon 6

Kamanue

Kamanue was a member of the Twelve Kizuki, having held the new position of Lower Rank Six.Kamanue was a very anxious and restive individual. He constantly wore a scared expression due to the fact that he felt weak and vulnerable as the lowest of the Lower Ranks. Little was known about Kamanue's strength and abilities as a demon, but it can be assumed that he was much stronger than the average demon and likely surpassed Kyogai, the former member of the Twelve Kizuki whose place he took.
